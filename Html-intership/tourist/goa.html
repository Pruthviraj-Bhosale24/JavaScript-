Goa Tourism Places

1. Baga Beach

Baga Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Goa. It is famous for water sports, nightlife and beautiful scenery.

2. Calangute Beach

Calangute Beach is known as the Queen of Beaches. It attracts tourists from all over the world.

3. Basilica of Bom Jesus

Basilica of Bom Jesus is a famous church and a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Old Goa.
