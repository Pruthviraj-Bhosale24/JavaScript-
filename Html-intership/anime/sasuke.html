Biography

Sasuke Uchiha is one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha Clan. After witnessing the destruction of his clan, he devoted his life to becoming stronger and seeking revenge. Later, he learned the truth about Itachi and fought alongside Naruto to save the ninja world.

Dream

Sasuke wanted to restore the honor of the Uchiha Clan and create a better future for the ninja world.
