Karnataka Tourism Places

1. Mysore Palace

A magnificent royal palace in Mysuru. It is famous for its beautiful architecture.

2. Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site with ancient temples and ruins. It reflects the glory of the Vijayanagara Empire.

3. Jog Falls

One of the highest waterfalls in India. It is a popular destination for nature lovers.
