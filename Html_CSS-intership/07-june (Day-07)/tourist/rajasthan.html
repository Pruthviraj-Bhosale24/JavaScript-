Rajasthan Tourism Places

1. Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal is a famous palace in Jaipur. It is known for its unique architecture and windows.

2. Amer Fort

Amer Fort is a magnificent fort located in Jaipur. It showcases Rajput architecture and history.

3. City Palace

City Palace is a historic palace complex located in Jaipur. It is one of Rajasthan's major tourist attractions.
